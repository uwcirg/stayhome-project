StayHome

Many people are concerned about or at risk for developing COVID-19. StayHome uses proven digital health strategies to engage concerned and at-risk community members through symptom tracking and access to reliable information. StayHome can also augment traditional public health methods for identifying and managing persons under investigation (PUIs) and their contacts, reducing the burden of labor-intensive workflows.

Why StayHome

The COVID-19 pandemic is straining existing public health processes and workflows. Many community members may be concerned about developing COVD-19. To meet this need we have developed StayHome, an app to help people who are staying home to minimize any risk they might present to others do things like track their symptoms and temperature, connect with relevant information and resources, and maintain a diary of people with whom they have had contact. We hope the app might also help people and public health connect more easily, when needed, in a situation where public health resources may be stretched thin.

The Features

Knowledge is power. Tracking your symptoms puts the ability to monitor possible coronavirus infection into your own hands. Our informational resources deliver trusted content to keep you and your loved informed and prepared.

Symptom Tracking

You can use StayHome to track your temperature and log upper respiratory symptoms (such as coughing and trouble breathing) you may experience. Tracking your symptoms gives you information you can act on.

Symptom Trends

StayHome takes the data you enter and shows you the trends in your symptoms over time. This helps you monitor your physical wellbeing as the outbreak continues.

Resources

StayHome includes a resource directory that gives you direct access to trusted information sources to support your need for accurate information about how to maintain physical, financial, and personal wellbeing during the outbreak.
